FAQ

Quick answers

Does CalAppt sync across devices?

Not automatically. CalAppt is saved in your browser on each device. Use Export Backup + Import/Merge to move your data between devices.

Will it send texts and emails for me?

CalAppt creates a draft and opens your Messages/Mail app. You review it and tap Send.

Can I still use Google Calendar?

Yes. Use the “Export Google (.ics)” option to bring your week into Google Calendar when you need it.

Where is my data stored?

On this device in your browser storage. Export a backup file if you want a copy you can keep.
