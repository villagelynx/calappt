Mobile Direction

What changes on phone

  • Customers and tasks become a simple picker instead of a wide desktop control area.
  • The calendar works better as a day view with quick week switching.
  • Scheduling becomes select item -> tap slot -> confirm.
  • Tasks stay light blue so they are visually different from customer sessions.
  • Backup import/export stays available from the top tools.
